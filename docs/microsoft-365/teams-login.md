# Logging In to Microsoft Teams

**Audience:** YSE staff and volunteers who have been granted Microsoft 365 access  
**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## Prerequisites

Before you can log in, an administrator must have granted your account access to the YSE Microsoft 365 tenant. You will receive an email notification when this is done.

If you have not received that notification but believe you need access, contact **support@youthsecurity.org**.

---

## Step-by-Step Login

### 1. Open the Teams web app

Navigate to **[https://teams.cloud.microsoft/](https://teams.cloud.microsoft/)** in your browser.

!!! tip "Prefer the desktop app?"
    You can also download the Microsoft Teams desktop app for Windows or macOS. When prompted to sign in, follow the same steps below.

---

### 2. Enter your YSE email address

Type your YSE email address (`yourname@youthsecurity.org`) in the sign-in field and click **Next**.

---

### 3. Sign in with Google

Microsoft will detect that YSE uses Google as its identity provider and redirect you to the **Google sign-in page**. Sign in with your YSE Google account as you normally would (password, two-factor authentication if prompted).

---

### 4. Trust the organization (first login only)

On your very first login, Microsoft may display a screen asking you to grant the YSE Microsoft tenant permission to read basic profile information from your Google account.

Click **"Trust organization"** (or **"Accept"**) to proceed.

!!! info "Why is this prompt shown?"
    This is a standard OAuth consent step. It allows the YSE Microsoft 365 tenant to confirm your identity via your Google account. No additional permissions beyond your basic profile are requested.

---

### 5. You are in

You should now be signed in to Microsoft Teams and see the main interface. Use the search bar at the top to find colleagues and start a conversation.

!!! note "Pilot phase"
    Teams access is currently being rolled out in stages. In this phase, only a limited number of YSE members have been granted access. If someone you are looking for cannot be found, they may not yet be on the platform.

---

## Troubleshooting

### The page does not redirect to Google

Make sure you entered your `@youthsecurity.org` address in step 2. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and try again in a private/incognito window.

### I see "Account not found" or "No account with that email"

Your account may not yet have been provisioned. Contact **support@youthsecurity.org** with your full name and YSE email address.

### I see "Need admin approval"

Your account exists but has not yet been granted a Teams license. Contact **support@youthsecurity.org**.

### I am stuck in a redirect loop

This can happen if you are already signed in to a personal or non-YSE Microsoft account in the same browser. Try signing in from a private/incognito window, or sign out of any personal Microsoft accounts first.

---

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issue not covered above, contact the YSE IT team at **support@youthsecurity.org** and include:

- Your YSE email address
- A brief description of what happens (and at which step)
- A screenshot if possible
