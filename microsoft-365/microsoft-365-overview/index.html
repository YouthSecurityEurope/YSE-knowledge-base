    Your Microsoft 365 Licence

    Audience: YSE staff and volunteers with Microsoft 365 access
    Last updated: June 2026

    What Is Included

    Your YSE Microsoft 365 licence gives you access to a broad set of Microsoft tools beyond Teams. This page summarises what is available so you can make the most of your licence.

    Core Office Web Apps

    These run entirely in your browser; no installation required.

    App What it does
    Word Create and edit documents
    Excel Spreadsheets and data analysis
    PowerPoint Slide presentations
    OneNote Digital notebooks and note-taking
    Outlook Web-based email (connected to Exchange Online)

    Access them at microsoft365.com and sign in with your YSE credentials (follow the same Google SSO login process as for Teams).

    Communication and Collaboration

    App What it does
    Microsoft Teams Chat, video meetings, calls, and file collaboration. See the Teams guide
    Exchange Online Business email with a 50 GB mailbox and calendar management
    SharePoint Online Team sites for document libraries and intranet pages
    OneDrive for Business 1 TB of personal cloud storage per user
    Microsoft Stream Video sharing and storage, integrated with Teams
    Viva Engage (formerly Yammer) Enterprise social networking

    Productivity and Task Management

    App What it does
    Microsoft Planner Kanban-style task boards for team projects
    Microsoft To Do Personal task lists, synced across devices
    Microsoft Lists Structured information tracking and smart lists
    Microsoft Forms Surveys, quizzes, and polls
    Microsoft Bookings Appointment scheduling (for external-facing bookings)
    Microsoft Loop Collaborative workspaces and reusable components
    Microsoft Shifts Staff scheduling within Teams

    Content and Learning Tools

    App What it does
    Microsoft Sway Digital storytelling, newsletters, and visual reports
    Microsoft Clipchamp Browser-based video editing
    Microsoft Editor AI-powered writing and grammar assistance
    Viva Learning Hub for training and learning content
    Viva Insights (Personal) Privacy-protected productivity analytics
    Microsoft 365 Copilot Chat AI chat with web grounding and writing assistance

    Getting Access to a Specific App

    Most apps are available immediately once your licence is activated; just sign in at microsoft365.com.

    If you cannot access a specific app or receive an "access denied" error, contact support@youthsecurity.org with the name of the app you are trying to use.
